About Us

Our Mission

The Ibn Al Haitham Technical High School in Ouarzazate aims to train future higher-level technicians. The school offers Bac+2 level programs, which are completed in 2 years after obtaining the baccalaureate. Upon graduation, the graduates have the option to enter the professional world or continue their studies to obtain a Bac+3 level (bachelor's degree) or higher (master's, engineering program, etc.).

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading institution in the field of DSI, recognized for our excellence in education, research, and innovation. We strive to empower our students to become passionate and ethical professionals who can drive technological advancements and positively impact the communities they serve.
